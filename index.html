What We Offer

Comprehensive Care
for Every Need

From skilled nursing to memory care and rehabilitation — Bhagirathi Nursing Home provides holistic, personalized services under one roof.

🏥

Skilled Nursing Care

24/7 professional nursing services including medication management, wound care, and health monitoring by licensed nurses.

🧠

Memory & Dementia Care

Specialized environment and programming designed for residents with Alzheimer's and other memory-related conditions.

🦽

Rehabilitation Services

Physical, occupational, and speech therapy to help residents regain strength and independence after illness or surgery.

🌸

Palliative & Hospice

Compassionate comfort-focused care to improve quality of life for residents and support for their families during difficult times.

🍽️

Nutrition & Dining

Chef-prepared nutritious meals tailored to dietary needs, served in a warm dining atmosphere that feels like home.

🎨

Activities & Wellness

Enriching social programs, arts, music therapy, gardening, and exercises to keep minds and bodies active and joyful.
